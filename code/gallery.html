History of design

This is a poster whose rug design is inspired by the art movement ART NOUVEAU.
The concept behind the rug design is that Art nouveau uses extensive arches and curved forms. Its lines are inspired by lines and shapes of nature. A sinuous curve was a common feature in this period’s art and architecture. The color palette of this period was subtle, shades of nude and not much loud.
